Covid 20-Pro

Stay Safe, Stay Home

As the COVID-19 virus spreads around the world, anxiety is high. Citizens everywhere are looking for ways to keep themselves and their families safe and healthy.

Protect Now

Take Steps To Protect Yourself

Wear A Face Mask

Cover your mouth, nose and chin. Press the nose strip against you nose bridge, face and under your eyes. The mask should not hang under your chin or around your neck, even when eating or drinking.

Learn More

Wash Your Hands

Wash or sanitise your hands before touching your mask. Wash or sanitise your hands before removing the mask. Wash or sanitise your hands after removing the mask.your hands well and often to avoid contamination.

Learn More

Avoid Close Contact

Stay 6 feet apart from people who don't live in your household. Remember that some people without symptoms may be able to spread the virus. Keeping distance from others is especially important for people.

Learn More

What Are The Main Symptoms?

People with COVID-19 have had a wide range of symptoms reported – ranging from mild symptoms to severe illness. Symptoms may appear 2-14 days after exposure to the virus. Anyone can have mild to severe symptoms. People with these symptoms may have COVID-19:

  • Fever or chills
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Headache
  • New loss of taste or smell
  • Sore throat
  • Congestion or runny nose
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Diarrhea
